Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AcquisitionPRO collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Income Goal Calculator.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or use the Income Goal Calculator, we may collect:

  • Name, mailing address, email address, telephone number, and fax number
  • Account credentials (securely encrypted passwords)
  • Professional role information (commercial real estate broker or investor)
  • Goal calculations, activity data, and performance metrics you enter
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Communication preferences and subscription settings

Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain non-personal information about how you use our service:

  • IP address, browser type and version, operating system
  • Device information and screen resolution
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Features used, calculations performed, and actions taken
  • Referral sources and search terms used to find our site

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and non-personal information to:

  • Provide our services: Calculate your income goals, track daily activities, and display progress dashboards
  • Account management: Create and manage your account, authenticate access, and maintain user profiles
  • Payment processing: Handle subscription payments, billing, and financial transactions
  • Customer support: Respond to inquiries, provide assistance, and resolve technical issues
  • Service improvement: Research, develop and improve programs, products, services and content
  • Communications: Contact you with information we think may be of interest, including service updates and relevant opportunities
  • Legal compliance: Meet our legal obligations and protect our rights and users' safety

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Premier Apartment Services, LLC dba AcquisitionPRO™ will not share your personal information with unrelated third parties without your permission, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our service, including hosting, payment processing, email delivery, and customer support
  • Business transfers: In connection with a corporate sale, merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
  • With your consent: When you have given us permission to share your information for a specific purpose

Data Security

We implement standard technology and operational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and system updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Employee training on privacy and security best practices

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and correction: You may access, correct, update, or delete your personal information through your account profile
  • Opt-out: You may unsubscribe from promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in emails
  • Data portability: Pro users can export their data in machine-readable formats
  • Account deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Cookie control: You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit functionality

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Our website uses cookies and web beacons (also known as "action tags" or "single-pixel gifs") to:

  • Remember your login status and user preferences
  • Analyze site usage patterns and performance
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Deliver relevant advertisements (through third-party advertising partners)

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies. However, you may not be able to use certain features of our website if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with trusted third-party providers that have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
  • HighLevel: CRM integration for contact management and lead tracking
  • Third-party advertising: Our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons to deliver relevant ads

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Children's Privacy

We will not intentionally collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

International Users

Our service is hosted in the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different privacy laws than your jurisdiction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of material changes by email or through prominent notices on our website. Your continued use of our service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
